What is Blockchain?

Blockchain is a digital ledger that records transactions across decentralized networks. It enables trust without middlemen, powering transparency and programmable value exchange. PTUSD relies on blockchain to offer community-driven stablecoin access.

×

Wallet Literacy

Wallets are your gateway to the decentralized economy. Knowing how to set up, secure, and use crypto wallets is fundamental. Holding BRC, BXC, or PTUSD requires wallet literacy to engage in lending, voting, and rewards.

×

DeFi vs TradFi

Traditional Finance (TradFi) is institution-led. DeFi removes gatekeepers. With tokens like VRB for identity, and BRC for community finance, you can join or even launch financial tools peer-to-peer.

×

How Crypto Powers the People

Crypto enables people to become stakeholders, not just customers. PTUSD empowers communities to circulate value locally. BXC fuels education and business. VRB makes ID programmable and secure.

×

DAOs & Community Governance

DAOs (Decentralized Autonomous Organizations) allow the people to govern themselves. BRC and BXC holders may vote on budgets, rewards, or partners. Blockchain = transparent democracy.

You can earn in Web3 through learning, liquidity provision, and staking. Bridge X Live rewards learners with BRC and BXC. These tokens can access services, unlock certifications, or grow in value.
